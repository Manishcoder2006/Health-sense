Health Recommendations

AI-curated lifestyle, diet, and preventive care tips based on your health profile.

Daily Exercise

Aim for 30 minutes of moderate activity daily. Walking, swimming, or cycling can boost your immune system and reduce stress.

Quality Sleep

Maintain 7–9 hours of consistent sleep. A regular schedule helps your body repair and strengthens immunity.

Stress Management

Practice mindfulness, deep breathing, or meditation for 10–15 minutes daily to lower cortisol levels.

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily. Proper hydration supports every system in your body.

Eat More Vegetables

Fill half your plate with colorful vegetables. They provide essential vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants.

Vitamin C Rich Foods

Include citrus fruits, bell peppers, and berries to boost your immune system naturally.

Omega-3 Fatty Acids

Consume fatty fish, walnuts, or flaxseeds 2–3 times per week to reduce inflammation.

Limit Processed Foods

Reduce intake of processed sugars, trans fats, and excessive sodium for better overall health.

Regular Health Screenings

Schedule annual check-ups and age-appropriate screenings to catch potential issues early.

Stay Up to Date on Vaccines

Ensure your vaccinations are current, including annual flu shots and any recommended boosters.

Hygiene Practices

Wash hands frequently, maintain dental hygiene, and practice safe food handling to prevent infections.

Mental Health Check-ins

Monitor your mental well-being regularly. Don't hesitate to seek professional support when needed.

Disclaimer: These recommendations are general wellness suggestions. Always consult your healthcare provider before making significant changes to your lifestyle or diet.
